## Desktop

**Resolution:** 1024 x 768 – 5120 x 2880
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (most common), 4:3 (older models)
**Orientation:** Landscape (primary), Portrait (supported)
**Pixel Density (ppi):** Low to High (100 – 300+)

> Example devices: HP EliteBook, ZBook, Panasonic CF-19, CF-20

## Tablet

**Resolution:** 768 x 1024 – 2560 x 1600
**Aspect Ratio:** 4:3, 16:9
**Orientation:** Portrait (common), Landscape
**Pixel Density (ppi):** Medium (170 – 350)

> Example devices: iPad, Samsung Galaxy Tab, Microsoft Surface

## Mobile

**Resolution:** 320 x 480 – 1440 x 3200
**Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (common), 4:3 (older models)
**Orientation:** Portrait (primary), Landscape
**Pixel Density (ppi):** High (260 – 600+)

> Example devices: iPhone, Samsung Galaxy S series, Google Pixel
